My Last Three Months

  • by:Shreya
  • date:September 11,2018

The pampered, youngest daughter of my family, calling the shots in the house with regards to meals, TV shows, movies, vacations and everything under the sun, was practically thrown right under the bus, three months ago. Just when the toil put in to get into a B-School seemed to be coming to fruition, reality struck me like lightning. Preparing for the entrances was the easy part – it was about to get a whole lot harder. The last three months have been tireless, to say the least. The academic rigour, assignments, presentations, sleepless nights, and the need for juggling everything are actually even more exhausting than they sound. This, hand in glove with the occasional pangs of homesickness, is enough to send the best of us spiralling.

reality struck me like lightning

These past three months can be best described as a precap into the corporate lifestyle. The pace is not getting any slower, that’s for sure. Amidst all the hullabaloo that is B-School life, the amount of knowledge I have gained is immense. My growth trajectory has literally shot up exponentially. What I have learned about people, interpersonal relationships, the value of collaboration, the need for pulling your own weight and a sense of responsibility and commitment, is truly unparalleled. I don’t know about any other discipline, but this degree teaches you a lot more than what you signed up for. And that to me, is absolutely precious.
