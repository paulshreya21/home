Triumph and Distaster are the same

  • by:Shreya
  • date:September 11,2018

Back in 2015, when I was applying to different colleges for Economics, I lost my morale after a point. I did not qualify for my top college choices. I was stressed and upset to the point of getting sick and not being able to appear for one of the entrance examinations I had been preparing hard for. This phase was perhaps my lowest, not because I did not qualify for some colleges, but because I was unable to handle it maturely. And this, I consider to be a major failure.

And in this life garbed in multiple hues, rose forms the most potent one as no matter how tough the going gets, having a rose-tinted view is what keeps us going and more importantly, keeps us happy, healthy and powerful. Such imperfect, yet rosy happiness is what beauty is all about.
