A Brand For Me.

  • by:Shreya
  • date:September 11,2018

I find myself resonating with Garnier. It is a brand which literally asks you to “Take Care”. The whole vibe of the brand is natural and attainable beauty standards. And this message of pure, healthy and natural beauty is conveyed loud and clear through the use of light and pastel colour schemes in its advertisements and the use of ingredients such as honey, shea butter, avocado, coconut oil and the likes, which we usually associate with health and nutrition. More than trends and concealers, Garnier focusses on sustainability and healing. It offers solutions to nourish your hair and skin. Innovation is what drives Garnier. It is constantly striving to develop technology that not only offers you healthy solutions, but are also environmentally sustainable and responsible.

This is one brand I feel a deep connect with. Its carefree, natural and holistic viewpoint calls out to me and inspires me to seek beauty and confidence in nature and health.
